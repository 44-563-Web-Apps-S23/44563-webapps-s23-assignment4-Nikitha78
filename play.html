Nikitha Ravella snips a play

Author : Percy Mackaye
playname : SAM AVERAGE
JOEL

[_With a singing call._] Four o'clock!--All's well! [_Jumping down from the embankment, he approaches the fire._


ANDREW

By God, Joel, it's bitter.


JOEL

[_Rubbing his hands over the coals._] A mite sharpish.


ANDREW

[_Looks up eagerly._] What?


JOEL

Cuts sharp, for Thanksgivin'.


ANDREW

[_Sinks back, gloomily._]

Oh! [_A pause._] I wondered you should agree with me. You meant the weather.


JOEL

Well, Andy, what'd you mean?
